## How to Read This

- **R** = Responsible (does the work)
- **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome)
- **S** = Supportive (helps with execution)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before decision)
- **I** = Informed (notified after decision)

## Decision Rights

### Strategy Division Decides:
- Market positioning
- Competitive response
- Go-to-market approach
- Pricing recommendations (leadership approves)

### Creative Division Decides:
- Brand voice and tone
- Campaign creative direction
- Visual identity application
- Copy style and frameworks

### Growth Division Decides:
- Channel mix and budget allocation (within approved budget)
- Test priorities and methodology
- Conversion tactics
- Growth loop mechanics

### Content Division Decides:
- Editorial calendar and cadence
- Content format and length
- Distribution timing and channels
- Repurposing priorities

### Analytics Division Decides:
- Measurement methodology
- Reporting format and cadence
- Statistical significance thresholds
- Data source selection
